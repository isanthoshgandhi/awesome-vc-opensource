## Market Sizing Framework

```
TAM / SAM / SOM — The Three Market Lenses

TAM (Total Addressable Market)
└── Total market demand if 100% market share
    └── Method: Top-down (industry reports) or Bottom-up (unit x price)

SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
└── Segment you can realistically target given your model
    └── Geography, customer type, willingness to pay

SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
└── Realistic market share in 3-5 years
    └── Usually 1-10% of SAM in early years

VC Rule of Thumb:
├── TAM must be > $1B for venture-scale returns
├── SAM should be large enough for $100M+ revenue company
└── SOM should show credible path to $10M ARR in 3-5 years

Research Sources (Free):
├── SEC EDGAR: Public company financials and 10-K market data
├── US Census Bureau: Industry statistics
├── Google Trends: Search demand signals
├── LinkedIn: Total addressable employee count as proxy for market
└── Job postings: Growth signal for companies and sectors
```
