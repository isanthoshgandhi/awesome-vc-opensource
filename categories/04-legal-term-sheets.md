## Quick Reference: SAFE vs Priced Round

```
SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)
├── Pre-Money SAFE: Converts before new money counted
├── Post-Money SAFE: Converts after new money (YC standard post-2018)
├── Key terms: Valuation Cap | Discount Rate | MFN | Pro Rata
└── Best for: Pre-seed, seed — quick, low-cost to execute

Convertible Note
├── Is debt (has maturity date + interest rate)
├── Key terms: Interest Rate | Maturity | Cap | Discount
└── Best for: Bridge rounds, when debt structure preferred

Priced Round (Preferred Stock)
├── Series Seed, Series A, etc.
├── Sets definitive pre-money valuation
├── Key terms: Liquidation preference | Anti-dilution | Board seats
└── Best for: Seed ($1M+), Series A and beyond
```
