## Key Portfolio Metrics to Track

```
Fund-Level KPIs
├── TVPI (Total Value to Paid-In) = (Unrealized + Realized) / Invested
├── DPI (Distributions to Paid-In) = Realized / Invested
├── RVPI (Residual Value to Paid-In) = Unrealized / Invested
├── IRR (Internal Rate of Return)
└── MOIC (Multiple on Invested Capital)

Company-Level KPIs (by stage)
├── 🌿 Seed: MRR growth, burn rate, runway, product engagement
├── 🚀 Series A: ARR, NRR, CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, gross margin
├── 📈 Series C: Revenue growth rate, EBITDA margin, NPS, market share
└── 🏢 Late Stage: EBITDA, free cash flow, IPO readiness metrics
```
