## The Winning Pitch Structure

```
Seed Deck — Ideal Slide Order (10-12 slides max)

1. Title  — Company name, tagline, contact
2. Problem  — Big, painful, frequent problem (with data)
3. Solution  — Your product (show, don't tell — demo screenshot)
4. Why Now  — Timing tailwind (tech shift, regulation, behavior change)
5. Market  — TAM > $1B, SAM, SOM with bottoms-up math
6. Business Model  — How you make money, pricing, ACV
7. Traction  — Revenue, users, growth rate, key customers
8. Competition  — 2x2 matrix — why you win
9. Team  — Founder backgrounds + why this team wins
10. Financials  — 3-year projections, burn, runway post-raise
11. The Ask  — How much, how you'll use it, milestones achieved
```
